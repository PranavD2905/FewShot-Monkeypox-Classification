🔬 CLINICAL INTERPRETATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
False Negatives (FN) - Most Critical for Healthcare:
  • EfficientNet-B0: Only 97 missed infections (best)
  • MobileNetV3: 174 missed infections (still acceptable)
  • Standard classifiers: Higher FN rate on limited data

False Positives (FP) - Resource Impact:
  • EfficientNet-B0: Only 61 false alarms (excellent)
  • Standard classifiers: Zero FP but at cost of higher FN

Recommendation:
  → EfficientNet-B0 for high-accuracy screening
  → MobileNetV3 for mobile/edge deployment with real-time constraints
